About Me

I am a fresh Master’s degree graduate in Computer Science from King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST). I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering with honors from King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM).

During my undergrad, in our senior project, we developed mobile applications on three platforms, IOS, Andriod and Windows Phone, using three different programming languages that help in managing large events and interacting with the audience. KFUPM awarded our project as the best Software Engineering Project for the Academic Year 2014-2015.

During my Master’s, we have prepared a survey on graph node ranking approximation methods and conducted a comprehensive experimental study on realistic large graphs with up to 126M edges that are orders of magnitudes larger than what are presented in the literature. Moreover, we have managed to scale the best-known ranking algorithm to 96,000 CPU cores on SHAHEEN II, a supercomputer at KAUST, to compute the exact nodes ranking on the large graphs.
